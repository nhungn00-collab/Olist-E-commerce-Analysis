# Olist E-commerce Data Analysis
 End-to-end e-commerce analysis focusing on customer behavior and revenue growth.
This project analyzes customer behavior and business performance on the Olist e-commerce platform using exploratory data analysis and customer segmentation techniques.

##  Overview
- Dataset: Olist e-commerce dataset (~99K orders)
- Revenue: ~$13.5M
- Focus: Revenue trends, customer segmentation, product performance

##  Key Insights
- Revenue shows strong growth from 2017, indicating successful business scaling
- Peak sales occur during seasonal events (e.g., Black Friday)
- Top categories (Beauty, Gifts, Home) dominate overall revenue
- AOV varies significantly across product categories and payment methods

##  Customer Segmentation
- Best Customers (~10.7%) have high spending but low purchase frequency  
- Churn Customers (~11.9%) mostly purchase only once  
- Key issue: failure to convert first-time buyers into repeat customers  

##  Business Impact
Improving retention strategies and targeting first-time buyers can significantly increase long-term revenue.  
Leveraging seasonal campaigns and optimizing product mix can further drive business growth.

##  Tools Used
- Python (Pandas)
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Customer Segmentation (RFM)
- Data Visualization
